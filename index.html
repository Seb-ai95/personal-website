A bit about me

I've spent the better part of a decade in B2B sales and go-to-market, from first rep on the floor, to co-founding companies, to carrying a bag at HubSpot. These days I'm obsessed with the agentic layer between GTM strategy and distribution: systems where AI does the grunt work and humans do the closing. On any given day that's four terminal tabs of Claude Code, far too many Clay tables, and a Notion agent that reads my inbox so I don't have to. Dad and recovering printer troubleshooter.
